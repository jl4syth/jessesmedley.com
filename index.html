Hi, I'm a developer that loves to help others bring their passions to life!

A little bit about me.

I am a California girl to core. I spent the first ten years of my adult life working in sports medicine. Eventually I was exhausted and burnt out working 60-80 hours a week, so I began to look for a change. I came across coding and web design and remembered how much I loved it in my first computer science course at the age of 12. I signed up for my first class that week!

When I learned to build my first website it quickly became obvious that tech and web design was my ticket to the change in life that I wanted. Now I spend my days working to live instead of living to work. I enjoy helping others find the changes in life that they need in order to live it to the fullest and find their success.
